About Vinyl Vault

Vinyl Vault is a women owned establisment created by a Hunter College student named Emily Quindi.

In an era where music is just a click away, vinyl remains something truly special for music lovers. It's more than just sound—it's an experience. Being able to currate your own physical collection of your favorite albums, discographies, & more.

Our Mission

At Vinyl Vault, our mission is to bring the magic of vinyl to music lovers of all kinds. We believe that records are more than just a way to listen—they’re a way to connect with music on a deeper level. By curating a diverse selection of high-quality vinyl, we aim to preserve the timeless experience of analog sound while making it accessible to both seasoned collectors and new enthusiasts. Whether you’re rediscovering classics or discovering something new, Vinyl Vault is here to keep the spirit of vinyl alive—one record at a time.

Whether you're hunting for vintage pressings, modern reissues, or limited-edition releases, Vinyl Vault is here to help you build your perfect collection. Every record in our online store is handpicked with care, ensuring quality and authenticity.
