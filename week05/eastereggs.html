  • Easter Eggs
Taylor Swift is basically the queen of Easter eggs — she’s been dropping hidden clues in her lyrics, music videos, outfits, and even Instagram captions for years. Whether it’s a cryptic number, a blink-and-you’ll-miss-it symbol, or a not-so-random emoji, fans have learned to analyze everything. From hinting at album releases months in advance to teasing collaborations and track names, decoding Taylor’s Easter eggs has become a full-time hobby for Swifties. It’s all part of the fun — like a giant puzzle only she knows the full picture of.
Some examples are:
  1. A neon sign that says “Lover” appears in the background months before the album name was revealed.
  2. The name “Nils Sjöberg” (her old songwriting pseudonym) is on a gravestone — symbolizing the end of that era.
  3. Elevator buttons light up in a specific color sequence, hinting at the release order of Taylor’s Version albums. The purple "3" button? That’s Speak Now (TV).
